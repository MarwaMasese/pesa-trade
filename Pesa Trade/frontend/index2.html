FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes cross Matrix trading so unique and different ?

The CP-Matrix methodology is based on a mathematical framework that reflects the cyclical nature of price action, focusing specifically on consolidations, trading ranges and general sideways movements. Other popular methods mostly attempt to address the likelihood of momentous moves and breakouts, which are far less predictable, and therefore, far less profitable.

Does the CP-Matrix model applicable to any market or only a specific one?

The CP-Matrix is applicable to any type of market out there. Since most of our trading activities are involved with the Foreign-exchange market, that is mostly where the CP-Matrix program is explained and demonstrated.

What are the advantages of the CP-Matrix over other trading methods?

The first and most important advantage is of course the level of conviction and prediction accuracy. As opposed to the rest of the inefficient analysis methods out there, Cross-price Matrix provides a real and significant predictive value. Furthermore, because of its nature, the CP-Matrix method also provides a built-in and solid strong guidelines for risk and money management.

What is a reasonable training time frame to become profitable?

As always, that obviously depends on the intensity of the training as well as the trader’s background and prior experience. However, on average and under normal circumstances, full mastery of the Cross-price Matrix methodology and Price-action Trading Course could take up to a year or two, which includes a period of actively practicing the CP-Matrix principles in a real-live trading account. CP-Matrix program is NOT promising to turn you into a multi-millionaire overnight, because that notion simply doesn’t exist.

Is the Cross-price Matrix more suitable to a specific type of traders or trading styles?

The Cross-price Matrix method as described in Price-action Trading Course is more accurate and works much better when applied to swing trading style (medium to long term trends, i.e. multiple days and weeks) rather than intra-day trading. As with most cases in technical analysis, higher time frames are less noisy and thus, can be better analyzed and predicted.

What is the main purpose for using the Cross-price Matrix methodology?

The main purpose of using the Cross-price Matrix method, as you will learn in our Price-action Trading Course, is to establish a strong and solid predictive system that allows for accurate dissection of the market, while producing reliable and highly probable trading opportunities. This results in a system you can rely on to be consistent across different markets.

Are there any specific tools or indicators related to the Cross-price Matrix™ methodology?

As you will learn in the Price-action Trading Course, there are no additional indicators needed for the CP-Matrix method. However, there is a specific tool that is the building block of the whole methodology. Namely, that is the basic Fibonacci retracement tool which is adjusted by applying a unique set of values, according to the CP-Matrix specific principles.

What type of market behavior produces the best results when applying CP-Matrix?

Any type of market behavior is well suited for the CP-Matrix method. In fact, the CP-Matrix itself helps to determine what type of market behavior is in control, and provides the relevant guidelines and game plan for taking action.
